# Figure 1 — Universal Unification

PRL Figure 1: Thermodynamic quantization and scale-invariant unification map.

## Panels

- **(a)** Extracted Vc across 73 experiments, grouped by bonding chemistry: metals (Vc ≈ 0.10 V) to covalent networks (Vc ≈ 2.70 V).
- **(b)** Scale-invariant unification: E scales as 1/r across eight orders of magnitude, from nanoscale HfO₂ field-induced crystallization to macroscopic W and Pt wire flash.
